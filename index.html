// About Me

Coming from my previous background in Hotel Management, my pursuit of visual design began in my final-year capstone project, where my team pitched a travel-dating app "NottAlone" as a startup idea. To tangibly visualize the concept, I taught myself to design a UI demo on Figma. The positive feedbacks motivated me to explore even further.


During my study in Computational Linguistics, I have then shifted my focus towards language technologies. I gained hands-on experience in find-tuning Large Language Models for various Natural Language Processing tasks, and worked as a student web developer for one year. In the project, I developed a digital archive "Boe eno moto", making linguistic resources of an indigenous language Bororo from Brazil available online.


Across these experiences, I’ve developed an interest in the intersection of Software Development, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning, and I am motivated to continue growing in these areas.
